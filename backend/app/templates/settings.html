How the safety system behaves

  • Paper trading is the default. Weak or confusing setups are rejected.
  • Daily loss caps, per-market caps, and consecutive-loss stops all block stronger actions.
  • No martingale, no averaging down, and no leverage are allowed in this MVP.
  • If there is no valid edge after costs in the live Polymarket book, the app says so clearly.
